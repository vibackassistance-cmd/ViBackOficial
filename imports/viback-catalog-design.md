Design a modern, minimalistic catalog website for a brand called **ViBack**, a business that resells LEGO sets. The website should focus on displaying products and redirecting customers to WhatsApp to place orders.

STYLE AND COLORS:
Use a **black and white color palette** with high accessibility standards. The **contrast ratio between text and background must be greater than 7:1 (WCAG AAA)**.

Color system:

* Primary background: White (#FFFFFF)
* Primary text: Black (#000000)
* Secondary background sections: Light gray (#F5F5F5)
* Button color: Solid black (#111111) with white text
* Button hover: Dark gray (#333333)
* Frame / card borders: Neutral gray (#D9D9D9)

Design style:

* Minimalist
* Modern e-commerce catalog layout
* Large product images
* Clean grid system
* Rounded corners (8px)
* Soft shadows for product cards
* Lots of white space
* Mobile-first responsive design

Typography:

* Headings: Bold modern sans-serif (similar to Inter / Montserrat)
* Body text: Clean sans-serif (Inter / Poppins style)
* Clear hierarchy (H1, H2, H3, body)

NAVIGATION STRUCTURE:

Inicio
│
├── Catálogo
│   ├── Productos más vendidos
│   ├── Star Wars
│   ├── Technic
│
├── Cómo comprar
│
├── Sobre ViBack
│
└── Contacto / WhatsApp
  ├── Correo
  ├── WhatsApp
  ├── TikTok

HOMEPAGE LAYOUT:

Hero section:

* ViBack logo
* Headline: “Encuentra los mejores sets de LEGO”
* Short description
* Primary CTA button: “Pedir por WhatsApp”

Featured section:

* 3 product cards for best-selling LEGO sets
* Product image
* Name
* Set number
* Button: “Consultar por WhatsApp”

Categories section:

* Star Wars
* Technic
* Productos más vendidos

How to buy section:
Show 3 steps:

1. Explora el catálogo
2. Elige tu LEGO
3. Escríbenos por WhatsApp para pedirlo

Product catalog page:

* Grid layout (3–4 columns desktop)
* Product cards with:

  * Image
  * Name
  * Set number
  * Age recommendation
  * Button: “Pedir por WhatsApp”

Contact page:

* WhatsApp contact button
* Email contact
* TikTok link

Important UX requirement:
The website is **catalog only**, there is **no checkout**. Every product interaction should lead to a **WhatsApp conversation CTA**.

Include:

* Floating WhatsApp button
* Clean navigation header
* Footer with contact info and social links
* Responsive layout for mobile and desktop
